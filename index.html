Aditya Tomar

I'm a 3rd year undergraduate at UC Berkeley studying EECS.


My research broadly lies in machine learning systems with a focus on efficient inference. Currently, I'm working at BAIR under Prof. Kurt Keutzer. Previously, I had the pleasure of working under Prof. Abhinav Bhatele at PSSG on memory-efficient adaptive optimization algorithms and efficient parallel training methods for LLMs.
